Stephanie Granger

Leadership

Stephanie Granger focuses on advancing the use of Earth observations at the intersection of science, remote sensing, and water management to address real-world water challenges and decision-making needs.

She convenes scientists, water managers, and decision-makers to identify critical gaps in hydrologic science and water resources applications and to translate satellite observations and research into actionable information.

She previously served as Program Manager for the NASA Western Water Action Office at NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, where she helped shape NASA’s strategy for applying Earth science to water management challenges across the Western United States.

She also led two SERVIR projects focused on drought and agriculture in Eastern and Southern Africa and Southeast Asia, partnering with regional institutions to apply Earth observations to support food security and water management decisions.
