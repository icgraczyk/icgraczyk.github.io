Our Mission

The Western Water Collaborative integrates Earth observations and basin-scale science to support informed water action across the West.

We bring together satellite data, existing research, and water leaders to turn complex science into shared clarity for real-world decisions.

What We Do

To support informed water action at basin scale across the western U.S., we:

  • Convene scientists, water managers, and agencies around real decision needs.
  • Integrate Earth observations and existing science to clarify risk, tradeoffs, and uncertainty.
  • Support the next generation of water leaders through mentored, applied work on real-world challenges.

We align the best available evidence with decision timelines—helping teams build shared understanding and move confidently from data to action.

Our work is practical and decision-focused—scoped to real choices, grounded in credible science, and built to move at the pace of water decisions.
